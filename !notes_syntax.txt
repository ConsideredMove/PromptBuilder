Weight and Emphasis Syntax
Attention/Emphasis:

(word) - Increase attention by 1.1x
((word)) - Increase attention by 1.21x (1.1²)
(word:1.5) - Increase attention by 1.5x (more precise)
[word] - Decrease attention by 0.9x
[[word]] - Decrease attention by 0.81x (0.9²)
(word:0.8) - Decrease attention to 0.8x

Examples:

(beautiful face:1.3) - Emphasize beautiful face
[large hands] - De-emphasize large hands
((masterpiece)) - Strong emphasis on masterpiece quality

Step-by-Step Control
Prompt Scheduling:

[word:number] - Start word after step number
[word::number] - Stop word at step number
[word:word2:number] - Switch from word to word2 at step number

Examples:

[detailed:0.5] - Start "detailed" halfway through generation
[blurry::0.3] - Stop "blurry" at 30% of steps
[sketch:photograph:0.4] - Switch from sketch to photograph at 40%

Alternating Prompts

[word1|word2] - Alternate between words each step
[red|blue|green] - Cycle through multiple options

Special Tokens

BREAK - Forces a new chunk (useful for very long prompts)
Commas separate different concepts/elements

Advanced Techniques
Prompt Editing:

Combine multiple techniques: ((red hair:1.2):0.8)
Nested emphasis: (beautiful (blue eyes:1.1):1.2)
